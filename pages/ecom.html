Ecommerce Solution

We are the best ecommerce solution provider

Online trade "e-Commerce" has become a norm in recent years. Given a choice, many people would prefer to quickly buy products and services online from the comfort of their office / homes. e-Commerce has also made it possible for companies to extend their reach to customers across geographies. So, we have, say a Swedish company selling an anti-virus application to somebody in Malaysia at the click of a button; or an Indian florist selling her wares to a customer in Australia straight from the nursery.
Ruboid's e-Commerce portal / application development services enable you to realize the full potential of online sales by literally taking your products / services to the doorsteps of your customers anywhere in the world.
Accordingly, we employ the following development lifecycle to build e-Commerce portals, be it for B2B or B2C domains:
Single-sourcing and publication of content across channels / websites
Storage and versioning of content
Access control to ensure that only authorized personnel edit, delete, or post content Workflow and publication management
Application support and maintenance
We understand that e-Commerce portal development is a unique combination of website development and software development activities.
We also provide you with a choice of using open source or commercial platforms for development of e-Commerce portals.
